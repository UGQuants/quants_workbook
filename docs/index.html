UG Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences lab workbook

A step-by-step guide for conducting quantitative research with R

by J Rafael Verudzco Torres and Mark Wong

2024-12-06

Welcome

Welcome to the Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences lab!

This workbook is targeted to University of Glasgow students enrolled in the Undergraduate Quantitative Research Methods course of the School of Social & Political Sciences. The activities are designed for RStudio Cloud.

The book was written using R bookdown package based on the GitHub repository: https://github.com/rstudio/bookdown-demo.
